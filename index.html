Help & User Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to IRON MIND.

IRON MIND helps you organize your subjects, manage daily tasks, and monitor your progress in a simple and structured way.

Dashboard

The Dashboard provides a quick overview of your work. It includes:

  • Progress Circle
  • Motivational Quote Carousel
  • Missed Task Counter
  • Today's Tasks
  • Subject List

Subjects

Create a subject for every area you want to manage. Each subject stores its own tasks. Click a subject to view or edit its tasks.

Tasks

Every task includes:

  • Topic
  • Source
  • Date
  • Remarks
  • Status

Update task status regularly to keep your progress accurate.

Search

The Search page helps you quickly locate tasks. You can search across all subjects or search within a selected subject.

Statistics

The Statistics page summarizes your overall progress using charts, performance indicators, and insights. Use it regularly to review your progress and identify areas that need more attention.

Settings

The Settings page allows you to configure Auto-delete Tasks, change the Accent Color, read the User Guide, learn about IRON MIND, and send feedback.

Tips

  • Plan your work before starting.
  • Update task status immediately after completing a task.
  • Review your Statistics page regularly.
  • Consistent daily progress produces long-term results.

About IRON MIND

IRON MIND is a productivity application designed to help users organize their work with clarity, discipline, and consistency. Rather than being just another task manager, it combines subject organization, task management, daily planning, search, and performance analysis into a single workspace.

The application is built around a simple idea: staying organized should be easy, motivating, and visually engaging. Every feature is designed to encourage consistent progress and make it easier to keep track of both daily activities and long-term goals.

Small progress every day creates extraordinary results.

IRON MIND will continue to evolve with the goal of becoming a complete personal productivity companion.
